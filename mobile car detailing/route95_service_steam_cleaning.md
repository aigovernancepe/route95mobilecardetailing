## H2: How Long Does Steam Cleaning Take?

Steam cleaning takes about 45 minutes for most vehicles, with surfaces drying within 1-2 hours.

Steam cleaning actually uses very little water compared to shampooing. The high temperature means moisture evaporates quickly, so your car dries faster. This also makes steam cleaning safe for electronics, screens, and areas where you don't want excess water.

Steam kills 99% of bacteria, mold, and allergens without leaving chemical residue behind. It's the safest deep-clean option for your interior.

Fort Lauderdale's humidity creates perfect conditions for mold and mildew growth inside vehicles. Air conditioning systems pull in moisture. Wet shoes and swimsuits introduce water. Spills that don't dry completely become breeding grounds.

- **Removes odors at the source** — Steam penetrates where smells originate rather than covering them up.

- **Sanitizes hard surfaces** — Steering wheels, shifters, and buttons get truly clean, not just wiped.
