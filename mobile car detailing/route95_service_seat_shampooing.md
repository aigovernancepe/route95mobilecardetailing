## H2: Why Do Your Car Seats Need Shampooing?

Fabric seats absorb sweat, body oils, and spills that vacuuming alone cannot remove. Shampooing extracts what's trapped deep in the fibers.

In Fort Lauderdale, your seats face extra challenges. Sunscreen transfers from your skin and leaves oily residue. Sweat soaks in during hot summer months. Sand from beach trips works its way deep into the fabric. Over time, this buildup creates odors and makes seats look dingy even after vacuuming.

Signs your seats need shampooing:
- Visible stains from drinks, food, or sunscreen
- Musty or sour smell when you get in the car
- Fabric looks darker or discolored
- You can see the outline where people sit
- Allergies act up when driving

Coffee and sunscreen are the two most common stains we treat in Fort Lauderdale. Both require pre-treatment for complete removal.
