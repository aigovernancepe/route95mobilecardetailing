## H2: Why Does Your Leather Need Professional Cleaning?

Body oils, sunscreen, and dirt build up on leather surfaces and cause cracking, fading, and permanent staining if not removed.

Leather is skin, and like your own skin, it has pores. Those pores absorb whatever touches them. In Fort Lauderdale, that includes:

- Body oils and sweat from hot days
- Sunscreen that transfers from arms and legs
- Lotion and cosmetics
- Food and drink residue
- Dirt from hands and clothes

This buildup does two things. First, it makes leather look dirty and feel sticky. Second, it prevents conditioning products from absorbing properly. You can apply all the conditioner you want, but if the pores are clogged with grime, it just sits on the surface.

Signs your leather needs cleaning:
- Shiny or sticky feeling on seats
- Visible discoloration, especially on driver's seat
- Dark patches on armrests and door panels
- Leather feels stiff rather than supple
- Conditioner doesn't seem to absorb

## H2: Our Leather Cleaning Process

1. **Inspection** — We check your leather for wear, cracking, and problem areas that need extra attention.

2. **Gentle Agitation** — We apply Chemical Guys pH-balanced Leather Cleaner with a horse hair brush. The soft bristles lift embedded dirt from leather pores without scratching.

3. **Surface Cleaning** — We work the cleaner into the leather using circular motions, paying extra attention to high-contact areas like bolsters and armrests.

4. **Wipe Down** — Clean microfiber towels remove the dissolved grime and cleaner residue from the surface.

5. **Final Inspection** — We check for any remaining spots and address them before finishing.

Leather cleaning prepares your seats for conditioning. We recommend pairing both services for complete leather care, especially in Florida where sun exposure dries out leather faster than in northern climates.
