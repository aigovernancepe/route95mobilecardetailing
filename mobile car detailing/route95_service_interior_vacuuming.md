## H2: Why Do You Need Professional Interior Vacuuming?

Consumer vacuums lack the suction power and specialized attachments needed to remove debris from automotive interiors.

Car interiors have dozens of places where dirt collects:

- Between seat cushions and bolsters
- In seat track rails where seats slide
- Under seats in the carpet
- Inside door pockets and storage compartments
- Around pedals and footwells
- In cup holders and console areas

Your home vacuum might get the visible stuff, but the sand and dirt hiding in these spots stays behind. That debris keeps grinding against surfaces every time you move around, wearing out carpets and upholstery faster.

In Fort Lauderdale, sand is the biggest issue. A single beach trip can deposit sand into every corner of your interior. Those particles are sharp and abrasive. Left in place, they damage carpet fibers and scratch plastic trim.

Signs you need professional vacuuming:
- Gritty feeling when you run your hand on carpets
- Visible debris in seat crevices
- Sand or dirt falling out when you move seats
- Crumbs and debris in cup holders and pockets
- Musty smell from trapped organic matter

1. **Floor Mat Removal** — We take out all mats and clean them separately so we can reach the carpet underneath.

3. **Crevice Work** — Specialized attachments reach into seat tracks, between cushions, and into tight spaces around the center console.

4. **Surface Vacuuming** — High-powered extraction removes loose debris from all carpet and upholstery surfaces.

5. **Detail Areas** — We hit door pockets, cup holders, storage compartments, and other spots where debris collects.
